Accessibiliy Statement

This portfolio was built around the accessibility standards of the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG 2.1), to grant access to people with disabilities and to allow usability for all audiences. All pages and content herein are accessible without the use of a mouse through the use of a keyboard via the "tab", "enter" and arrow key buttons. All HTML5/CSS code was validated and tested for contrast and style errors with the use of the Web Accessibility Evaluation Tool, and successfully passed. All images contain alternate text to provide a conceptual framework for the visually impaired. Customized closed captioning for video content was developed by the creator with the help of Subtitle Edit Online Beta. Closed captioning offers a narrative for the auditory impaired while engaging visual content, and serves a vital role in accessibility.

Although Ryan Yerge has formulated his portfolio to the best of his ability to adhere to all accessibility standards and expectations, some areas of the site may not achieve this.
